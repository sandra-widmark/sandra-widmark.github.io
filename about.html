About me

I am 27 years old and live in Spånga in western Stockholm. I worked until recently full time at an e-commerce company, but now I have decided to invest fully in learning frontend development at Lernia. I have previously studied graphic design three years at Linköping University, which led to that my interest in web and development started to grow.

Other interests for me are health and exercise, and to cook great vegetarian and vegan food. I would be happy to teach you how to make a good vegan chia seed pudding or how a lasagna can be varied endlessly!
